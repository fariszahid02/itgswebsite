Orders Handling




For holiday season, Christmas, New Year, Valentine's Day etc. We will take more time to fulfil the parcel. Hope you understand it's a small scale business, a team of 2-4 person to gift wrapped hundreds of parcel a day. We will try our best to ship out asap.

We will always ship all the items in one parcel (ready stock and pre-order items) If you are urgent, please contact our support for further arrangement. If you need the ready stock items to be shipped first, you will have to bare the additional postage fees.

Return Policy




Customers can return damaged/wrong/faulty items (apart from sale items) within 7 days of receipt date for a refund of that item. Any lateness will not be entertained and item(s) will be returned back to the customers.

We take great measures to ensure our product quality but sometimes things go wrong during delivery. Please help us take a photo of your item (the part that is faulty) and include a short description along with your order ID and email support@sparklesbydurra.com. We will exchange your item for a new one and we will bear all delivery cost to return the faulty product to us and resend the new product to you. In the event your faulty item is out of stock, we will reimburse you monetary refund or replace with another code which has same value of total faulty / damage item received.

Sparkles by Durra will only accept items in their original condition i.e. UNWORN, UNUSED, UNALTERED, condition with ALL TAGS ATTACHED. Sparkles by Durra will not be responsible for returns that do not reach us and hence, we recommend you insure your parcel for its appropriate value.
